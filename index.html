About us

Techno Culture Portugal it’s a non-profit organization dedicated to promoting and expanding the culture of techno music in Portugal through events. Founded in 2019, our institution has been dedicated to creating unique musical experiences and supporting emerging artists in the electronic music scene. Nowadays our collective is composed of DJs, producers, organizations, artists and electronic music enthusiasts who share a common passion for Techno culture and music. We already organize a variety of events, from underground parties to outdoor festivals always aiming to provide unforgettable experiences for our participants.
